How does it work?

1

We have pre-made options and structures available for you. Select the most suitable option for you.

2

If still in doubt, you can:
a) Either answer a few questions about your business;
b) or talk to one of our experts.
Accordingly, our platform shall recommend the structure suitable to your business. Ofcourse, the decision is always yours!

3

Select the plan based on your requirements

4

Pay 20% price of the plan as an advance.

5

Start by uploading your documents and filling up the details required, our platform will guide you through it

6

Post-verification, our team will take care of the registration process and timely updates will be posted on the dashboard (access shall be given once documents and information is submitted). You can also select from our add-on services.

And Voila! There you have it. A fully compliant legal entity structure in a foreign land.
